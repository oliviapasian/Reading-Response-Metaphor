my website is a ball of yarn

I think a website could be like a ball of yarn. It could unspool as you go along and you’d have to keep unspooling to reach the end.

The website could curve in unpredictable ways or it could be pulled taut in a line. It could come apart quickly or slowly.

It could be any colour you want and maybe the colours change part of the way through. Maybe you weave it into a pattern, like knitting a sweater or crocheting a scarf.

You could even pull it apart again and start over. It’s anything you can shape out of a line.
